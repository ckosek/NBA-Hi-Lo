Who Had the Higher Career Average?

How to Play

  • Two NBA players are shown with a stat category — Points, Rebounds, or Assists per game.
  • Click the player you think has the higher career average for that stat.
  • Both players' actual averages are revealed after your pick.
  • A tie counts as a win for both.
  • Build the longest streak you can — your personal best is saved across sessions.
